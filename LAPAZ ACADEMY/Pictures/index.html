MISSION STATEMENT:

To invites a community atmosphere and a beautiful surroundings in a positive learning environment. To have proud academic tradition and maximise every learning opportunity to shape our students future through high quality, values enriched teaching and learning.

We have a clear vision and purpose. We would foster student excellence with the support and involvement of our learning community.

At Lapaz Nursery and Primary Schools, the school community works together to ensure every child has a positive, happy and rewarding school experience. Parents and teachers are genuine partners in the learning process, ensuring students are encouraged to participate, to strive to achieve their best, to br considerate and supportive of others, and to value the pursuit of knowledge.

Vision:

Our school empowers all students to embrace learning, achieve their personal best and build their emotional, social and physical well-being.

Our Purpose:

We provide an inclusive, caring and rigorous learning environment that engages our student and challenges them to grow academically, socially, emothionall, physically and creatively to their full potential. Student are encouraged to develop a social conscience which enables them to contribute to their family, school and the wider community.

School Philosophy:

We are dedicated to building upon a strong community relationship positive student achievements and seeking out new innovation to further support the thinking skills and creativity of our learning community in partnership with the community, we provide our students with the means and motivation to excel and leads us into an exicting future.

Enactment of the School Philosophy:

Our school operates by asking a fundamental question, "Is this work or process in line with the school vision, values and beliefs?" This question guides and directs the practice of all leaders in the school.

The vision of the school will sit at the center of our focus and there wil be an expectations about all work aligning with the vision. Regular evaluation processes will be in place to measure performances in all areas of the school will reflect the set of the school.
